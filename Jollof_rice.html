Jollof Rice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of long-grain parboiled rice
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 red bell peppers, blended into a puree
  • 2 tomatoes, blended into a puree
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of ginger, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (adjust to your spice preference)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 1/2 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of frozen peas and carrots

Instructions:

  1. Wash the rice and drain it. Set it aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  3. Saut√© the chopped onions until they become translucent.
  4. Add the minced garlic and ginger and cook for a minute until fragrant.
  5. Add the pureed red bell peppers and tomatoes, and cook for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally until the mixture thickens and the oil starts to separate.
  6. Stir in the thyme, curry powder, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
  7. Add the rice and stir to coat it with the tomato and pepper mixture.
  8. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth and bring to a boil.
  9. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let the rice simmer for about 20-25 minutes or until it's cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
  10. Stir in the frozen peas and carrots, and let them heat through.
  11. Fluff the Jollof Rice with a fork and serve hot.
