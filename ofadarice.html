Ofada Rice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of Ofada rice
  • 6 pieces of green bell peppers
  • 2 pieces of scotch bonnet peppers (or to taste)
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1/4 cup of palm oil
  • 1/2 cup of ground crayfish
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cayenne pepper (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Wash the Ofada rice and cook it in a pot of boiling water until it's tender. Drain and set it aside.
  2. While the rice is cooking, blend the green bell peppers, scotch bonnet peppers, onion, and garlic in a food processor until smooth.
  3. Heat the palm oil in a separate pot until it melts and becomes clear.
  4. Add the blended pepper mixture to the palm oil and cook for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Stir in the ground crayfish, salt, and cayenne pepper (if using) into the pepper sauce and let it simmer for another 5 minutes.
  6. Serve the Ofada rice with the pepper sauce on the side. You can also garnish with fried plantains or boiled eggs if desired.
