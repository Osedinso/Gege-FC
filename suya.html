Suya Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of beef or chicken, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup of peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons of ground cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of paprika
  • 1 tablespoon of ground ginger
  • 1 tablespoon of ground garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Skewers for grilling

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the peanut butter, cayenne pepper, paprika, ground ginger, ground garlic, vegetable oil, and a pinch of salt to make the suya spice paste.
  2. Thread the meat cubes onto skewers, and brush the suya spice paste generously over the meat.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Grill the skewers for about 15-20 minutes, turning them occasionally until the meat is cooked and has a nice char on the outside.
  5. Serve the suya hot with additional suya spice paste on the side, sliced onions, and tomato slices.
