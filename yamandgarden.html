Yam and Garden Egg Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium-sized yams
  • 6-8 fresh garden eggs
  • 1 onion
  • 2-3 fresh tomatoes
  • 2-3 Scotch bonnet peppers (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 2 tablespoons of palm oil
  • 1 teaspoon of ground crayfish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash and peel the yams, then cut them into small chunks.
  2. Boil the yam chunks in a pot of water until they are tender. Drain and set aside.
  3. Wash the garden eggs and remove the stalks. You can choose to leave the skin on or peel them, depending on your preference.
  4. In a separate pot, boil the garden eggs until they are soft and tender, which should take about 20-30 minutes.
  5. While the garden eggs are boiling, prepare the sauce. In a blender, combine the chopped onions, tomatoes, Scotch bonnet peppers, and blend until smooth.
  6. In a separate pot, heat the palm oil and fry the blended sauce until the oil starts to separate from the mixture.
  7. Add the boiled garden eggs to the sauce and stir to combine. Simmer for about 5-10 minutes.
  8. Add the ground crayfish, salt, and pepper to taste. Continue to simmer for a few more minutes.
  9. Serve the yam and garden egg sauce together, and enjoy your delicious meal!
