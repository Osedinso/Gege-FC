Delicious Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of cooked rice
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup diced mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers)
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the diced onions and garlic. Saut√© for 2-3 minutes until they become fragrant and slightly browned.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables to the skillet and stir-fry for about 3-4 minutes until they become tender.
  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and pour the beaten eggs onto the other side. Scramble the eggs until they are fully cooked.
  5. Add the cooked rice to the skillet and mix it with the vegetables and eggs.
  6. Drizzle soy sauce and sesame oil over the rice and stir to combine. Continue cooking for 2-3 minutes until everything is heated through.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir to evenly distribute the seasoning.
  8. Remove from heat and serve your delicious fried rice hot.
