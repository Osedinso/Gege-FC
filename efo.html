Fufu and Efo Riro Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of cassava fufu
  • 1/2 cup of red palm oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 red bell peppers, chopped
  • 2 green bell peppers, chopped
  • 2 cups of spinach, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of ground crayfish
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the palm oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until they become translucent.
  3. Add the minced garlic and stir for about a minute.
  4. Add the ground crayfish, cayenne pepper, and salt. Stir well.
  5. Add the red and green bell peppers and continue to cook for a few minutes.
  6. Stir in the chopped spinach and cook until wilted.
  7. Adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper as needed.
  8. In a separate pot, prepare the cassava fufu according to the package instructions.
  9. Serve the Efo Riro with a scoop of fufu and enjoy!
